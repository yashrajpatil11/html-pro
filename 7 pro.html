Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer who currently captains the India national cricket team across all formats. He is a right-handed batsman. Considered one of the best batsmen of his generation and one of the greatest opening batters of all time,. Under his captaincy India won the 2018 and 2023 Asia cups and 2018 Nidahas Trophy.
